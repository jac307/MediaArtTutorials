<h3 style="color: darkred;">[15‚Äì20 min] Sketching</h3>

You may sketch using a **digital or physical notebook**.  
According to Wikipedia, a sketch is ‚Äúa rapidly executed freehand drawing that is not usually intended as a finished work.‚Äù

#### Task:
- Sketch **three compositions** (one per format), each combining **two different Gestalt principles** from the list above.
- Explore how these principles interact visually to create structure and meaning.

#### Composition Formats (Choose one per sketch):
- **Graphic Design** ‚Üí Logo or Poster  
- **UI Design** ‚Üí One-page website mockup or App intro + second screen  
- **Photomontage** ‚Üí A photographic composition using collage and texture  

#### Focus on:
- Artistic experimentation ‚Äì explore **unexpected visual relationships**  
- Communication ‚Äì ensure Gestalt principles support **clarity and impact**  
- Negative space and layering ‚Äì enhance **visual hierarchy**  
- Viewer experience ‚Äì guide the eye and create **unity in complexity**  
- Colour & contrast ‚Äì use them to support perceptual grouping and focus  

> Ask the professor for feedback during sketching!
