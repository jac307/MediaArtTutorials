## Objective

Create **three digital compositions**, each combining **two different Gestalt Principles of Design** from the list below:  
1. Symmetry  
2. Connectedness  
3. Common Region  
4. Focal Point  
5. Common Fate  

<img src="imgs/22.png" style="width: 50vw; height: auto;">

Each composition should demonstrate how the selected principles interact to affect **form**, **structure**, and **visual perception**.  
Use what you‚Äôve learned so far (e.g., layout, grids, colour theory, texture) along with the **new Gestalt concepts and examples** introduced in class to produce designs that are **intentional**, **engaging**, and **format-aware**.

**Software** 
- **Adobe Illustrator** (vector-based) ‚Äì for general layout, shapes, and composition setup  
- **Adobe Photoshop** (pixel-based) ‚Äì for textures, photomontage, or image-based enhancements

- Artistic experimentation ‚Äì explore **unexpected visual relationships**  
- Communication ‚Äì ensure Gestalt principles support **clarity and impact**  
- Negative space and layering ‚Äì enhance **visual hierarchy**  
- Viewer experience ‚Äì guide the eye and create **unity in complexity**  
- Colour & contrast ‚Äì use them to support perceptual grouping and focus
